the dissociation constant of the enzyme-substrate complex, reflects stability of enzyme-substrate interaction

$$
K_{m}=\frac{k_{-1}+k_{2}}{k_{1}}
$$

$k_1$ = rate constant for formation of the enzyme-substrate complex
$k_{-1}$ = dissociation of enzyme-substrate complex
$k_{2}$ conversation of enzyme-substrate complex to product
