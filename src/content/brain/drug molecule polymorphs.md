**My initial thoughts**
Just from the name, poly = multiple, morphs = morphology = physical structure; likely when certain molecules come together to form a "new" compound, or change their configuration. This can either be good or bad, depending on the metabolism chain and what you want

**What it actually is**
Aka Drug polymorphism, it's when a chemical compound (molecule) arranges itself differently depending on its conditions. Polymorphic forms can change how the drug is metabolized and interacts with other compounds, which means it can alter the drug's *safety* and *efficiency*
