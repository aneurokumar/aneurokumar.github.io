> This MOC focuses on Lab Management and closely associated topics such as leadership, equipment, technology, protocols, and safety.

Lab Management is a huge part of my identity and career, and is a unique blend of research/engineering, operations, stakeholder management, writing, presenting, IT, and science. It involves a lot of technical knowledge of machines, chemicals, and different assays.

## Career Relevance
While interviewing for Lab Manager positions at startups, it's important for me to have some stake in the science in order to better serve as the lab manager. I don't need to be leading a project, but even participating in some of the work alongside the people.

For Biotech Incubators, I think looking at this from having varied experiences in different startups would be the best.

### Financial/Business
* [Execdr | Exploring the Intersection of Science & Business](https://www.excedr.com/blog): Lab operations and funding strategies for more Lab Director level position. More finance strategy related resources.
* [Run Your Lab Like a Business \| Lab Manager](https://www.labmanager.com/): Recommendations on different equipments and lab products, as well as lab management strategies for things like moving, scale-up, and team leadership
